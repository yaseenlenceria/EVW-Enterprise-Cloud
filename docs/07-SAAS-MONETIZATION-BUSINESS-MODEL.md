# EVW ERP - SaaS Monetization & Business Model

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete business model, pricing strategy, and monetization plan for transforming EVW ERP into a profitable, scalable SaaS platform for the global vape and retail industry.

---

## Business Model Overview

### Phase 1: Single-Client (EVW Pakistan) - Months 0-6
**Revenue Model**: One-time development + monthly maintenance
- Initial development: $70,000-100,000
- Monthly maintenance: $1,000-2,000
- **Goal**: Build proven system for EVW

### Phase 2: Early Adopters - Months 6-12
**Revenue Model**: Annual contracts with setup fees
- Setup fee: $2,000-5,000 per client
- Annual subscription: $5,000-15,000
- Target: 5-10 early adopter clients
- **Goal**: Validate product-market fit

### Phase 3: SaaS Platform - Month 12+
**Revenue Model**: Self-service subscription + add-ons
- Monthly subscriptions: $25-199/month
- Annual plans (2 months free)
- Add-on features
- **Goal**: Scale to 100+ customers

  - Basic features only

Present plans in this order:
```
ENTERPRISE    PROFESSIONAL    STARTER
  $199/mo       $75/mo        $25/mo
```

## Churn Reduction Strategy

### Target Churn Rate: < 5% monthly

#### 1. Onboarding Excellence (First 30 Days Critical)

**Days 1-7: Setup & First Invoice**
- Welcome email with video tutorial
- Live chat support available
- Goal: Create first invoice within 7 days
- Success rate: 80%+

**Days 8-14: Data Import**
- Guide to import products
- Offer free data migration service
- Goal: Import 50+ products

**Days 15-30: Team Adoption**
- Invite team members
- Role assignment
- Goal: 2+ active users

**Churn Risk**: 40% of users who don't create invoice in first 7 days will churn.

#### 2. Usage Monitoring & Intervention

**Red Flags** (trigger retention campaign):
- No login for 7 days
- No invoices created in 14 days
- Downgrade from higher tier

**Interventions**:
- Email: "We noticed you haven't logged in..."
- Offer: Free 30-min training call
- Discount: 20% off next month
- Survey: Why are you leaving?

#### 3. Feature Engagement

Track key actions:
- Invoices created
- Products added
- Customers added
- Reports viewed

**Power Users** (low churn):
- Create 10+ invoices/month
- Add 50+ products
- Use 3+ features regularly

**At-Risk Users** (high churn):
- < 5 invoices/month
- < 20 products
- Use only 1 feature

#### 4. Win-Back Campaign

For churned customers:
- Email 7 days after cancellation
- Offer: 50% off for 3 months
- Ask: What can we improve?
- Win-back rate: 10-15%

- Revenue: $12k
- Pricing: Custom agreement

- Revenue: $128k total
